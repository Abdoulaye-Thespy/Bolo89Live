About this role

Senior UI/UX Designer (Full time)

.
Job Summary:

We are seeking a talented and experienced Senior UX Designer to join our team and lead the user experience design efforts for our digital products. As a Senior UX Designer, you will play a crucial role in crafting intuitive, user-centered designs that enhance the overall usability and effectiveness of our digital platforms. Your expertise will guide the product design process, ensuring that our products meet and exceed the expectations of our users.

Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Human-Computer Interaction, Design, or a related field (Master's degree is a plus).
  • Proven experience as a UX designer, with a strong portfolio showcasing your work.
  • Expertise in user research, information architecture, interaction design, and usability testing.
  • Proficiency in design and prototyping tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, or similar.
  • Knowledge of front-end development and its impact on UX.
  • Familiarity with accessibility and web design standards.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills.
  • Leadership abilities and experience mentoring junior designers.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a user-centered mindset.
